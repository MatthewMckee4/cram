# Spaced Repetition

Cram uses the SM-2 algorithm. After reviewing a card, rate it:

| Rating | Meaning | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|
| 1 — Again | Complete blackout | Reset to 1 day |
| 2 — Hard | Correct with difficulty | Interval × 1.2 |
| 3 — Good | Correct with effort | Interval × ease |
| 4 — Easy | Perfect recall | Interval × ease × 1.3 |
